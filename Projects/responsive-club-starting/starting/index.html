About Halo Online

Halo is a well established game with a lot of history amongst Xbox fans. With the main character Master Chief, the player is dropped into a constant between many different lifeforms in the universe. Master Chief exists to prevent the rings called Halo from being activated and used to destroy the entire universe. As the halo franchise lives and breathes today, we invite you to the multiplayer experience. We have formed an online group of Halo enthusiasts to get involved with our online social club. Talk Halo, play Halo or just blow off some steam.

Meetings

Meetings happen everyday on our Halo Online discord server. We provide daily updates for our roadmap and where we see Halo Online heading in the not too distant future. Live meetings last around 1 hour and are usually recorded so you will never miss out.

Our discord server also have chat rooms for users to arrange online meetups where you can either play casually with other members online or play competively with some of our more competitive members in the Halo world.
